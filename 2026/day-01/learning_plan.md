## What is DevOps and Cloud Engineering?

DevOps is the practice of automating and streamlining how software is built,
deployed, and maintained in production. Cloud Engineering is the infrastructure
layer underneath — compute, networking, and storage that makes it all run.
Together they shift the mindset from "it works on my machine" to "it runs
reliably, at scale, for everyone."
